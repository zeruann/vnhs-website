#ChooseSMAW

In addition to the specialized training it provides, choosing the Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) strand offers numerous advantages for students interested in the field of welding and metalwork. This strand equips individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the welding industry, where their expertise in SMAW can contribute to the construction of infrastructure, manufacturing, and other sectors that rely on metalwork. Students in the SMAW strand learn a variety of welding techniques and practices. They develop proficiency in working with various metals, including steel, aluminum, and stainless steel, understanding their properties and how they behave during the welding process. Through practical training, students acquire hands-on experience in operating welding equipment, reading technical drawings, and executing welds with precision and accuracy.
