SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

Welcome to our senior high school web page! Here, you'll discover an educational institution dedicated to academic excellence, personal growth, and lifelong friendships. With passionate educators, modern facilities, and a comprehensive curriculum, we provide a transformative learning experience. Explore our programs, clubs, and support services as we empower students to unlock their potential and prepare for a successful future. Join us on this virtual journey to discover the vibrant opportunities that await you at our senior high school.
SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL OFFERS
Academic Track

The Academic Track at our school is a specialized program designed to provide students with a comprehensive and well-rounded education. Through a carefully curated selection of subjects, students in the Academic Track develop strong foundational knowledge in various disciplines, including Mathematics, Science, English, Social Sciences, and Languages.

Read More
Technical-Vocational-Livelihood

The TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood) Track at our school offers students an exciting and practical educational pathway that prepares them for the dynamic world of work. Through hands-on training and real-world applications, students in the TVL Track gain specialized skills in various vocational fields, such as culinary arts, computer technology, automotive mechanics, and entrepreneurship.

Read More
Arts and Design

The Arts and Design program provides a dynamic and immersive educational experience for students passionate about creativity and self-expression. Through this program, students explore various artistic disciplines such as visual arts, graphic design, photography, fashion design, and more. They engage in hands-on projects, honing their artistic skills and developing their unique artistic style.

Read More
Sports Track

The Sports Track offers a comprehensive and immersive educational experience for students who have a passion for athletics and physical fitness. This track provides specialized training in various sports disciplines, including team sports, individual sports, and fitness training. Students engage in rigorous physical conditioning, skill development, and strategic game play.
