SCIENCE DEPARTMENT

The Science Department is a captivating and inquiry-driven realm where students embark on a voyage of discovery, exploration, and understanding of the natural world. It is a place where curiosity thrives, and students engage in hands-on experiments, scientific investigations, and critical analysis. Through rigorous study of biology, chemistry, physics, and other scientific disciplines, students develop a deep appreciation for the principles that govern our universe. The department fosters a spirit of scientific inquiry, encouraging students to ask questions, challenge assumptions, and seek evidence-based explanations. With passionate instructors and state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, the Science Department equips students with the knowledge, skills, and scientific literacy necessary to comprehend complex phenomena, solve real-world problems, and contribute to advancements in scientific research and innovation.
