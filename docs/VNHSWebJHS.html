JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL

Welcome to our Junior High division, where the journey of knowledge and personal growth truly begins to take flight. At our school, we believe in providing a nurturing and engaging environment that empowers young minds to explore, discover, and excel. Junior High is a pivotal phase in every student's academic journey, where they embark on a path of self-discovery, intellectual curiosity, and character development. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing a comprehensive curriculum that encompasses a wide range of subjects, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning.

SPECIAL SCIENCE PROGRAM

The Special Science Program is a rigorous and enriching academic initiative designed for students passionate about scientific exploration and discovery. This program offers a comprehensive curriculum encompassing various scientific disciplines, including biology, chemistry, physics, mathematics, and computer science. Students engage in hands-on experiments, research projects, and scientific investigations, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and a deep understanding of scientific principles. The Special Science Program equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary to pursue advanced scientific studies.

SPECIAL PROGRAM IN THE ARTS

The Special Program in the Arts is a transformative learning opportunity for students with a deep interest and talent in the creative arts. This program provides a multidisciplinary approach, allowing students to explore various artistic disciplines, such as visual arts, performing arts, music, and creative writing. Through intensive training, exposure to renowned artists, and collaborative projects, students hone their artistic skills, foster their creativity, and gain a profound understanding of the artistic process. The Special Program in the Arts nurtures the next generation of visionary artists, empowering them to make a significant impact in the world of arts and culture.

SPECIAL PROGRAM FOR JOURNALISM

The Special Program for Journalism is an immersive educational experience designed to cultivate aspiring journalists' skills and passion for storytelling. Through a comprehensive curriculum, students explore various aspects of journalism, including news writing, investigative reporting, multimedia journalism, and ethical considerations. This program provides hands-on training, opportunities to work with industry professionals, and access to cutting-edge technology, enabling students to develop a strong foundation in journalism.

SPECIAL PROGRAM FOR SPORTS

The Special Program for Sports is an exceptional platform for talented athletes to pursue their athletic dreams while receiving a well-rounded education. This program strikes a balance between rigorous academic curriculum and intensive sports training, providing students with the necessary support to excel both on and off the field. The Special Program for Sports aims to develop athletes who not only achieve sporting excellence but also acquire vital life skills such as teamwork, resilience, and discipline, enabling them to succeed in their athletic pursuits and future endeavors.
