ADMISSIONS

Admissions Open for the Academic Year 2023-2024
We are excited to announce that admissions are now open for Valencia National High School (VNHS).
As a prestigious educational institution, VNHS is dedicated to providing a top-notch education and fostering the growth and success of our students.
Why choose Valencia National High School?
At VNHS, we strive for excellence in academics, character development, and holistic education.
Here's why you should consider joining our vibrant school community:

1. Exceptional Faculty

Our experienced and passionate teachers are committed to nurturing the talents and potential of each student. They create engaging learning environments and promote critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration.

2. Comprehensive Curriculum

VNHS offers a comprehensive curriculum aligned with national standards. From core subjects to a wide range of elective courses, we provide a well-rounded education that prepares students for future success.

3. State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our campus boasts modern classrooms, fully equipped science and computer laboratories, well-stocked libraries, and excellent sports facilities. We provide the resources and infrastructure necessary for a stimulating learning experience.

4. Holistic Development

We believe in the importance of a balanced education. In addition to academics, VNHS offers a variety of extracurricular activities, including clubs, sports teams, arts programs, and community service opportunities, fostering well-rounded growth.

5. Competitive Admissions Process

Our admissions process ensures fairness and objectivity. We carefully evaluate each applicant based on academic records, potential, and personal qualities to create a diverse and talented student body.
HOW TO APPLY?

1

Review Admission Requirements:

Familiarize yourself with the admission requirements for your desired grade level.

2

Prepare Application Documents:

Gather the necessary documents, such as academic transcripts, recommendation letters, and any additional requirements mentioned in the application guidelines.

3

Complete the Online Application:

Visit our website and fill out the online application form accurately and thoroughly.

4

Submit Application and Supporting Documents:

Upload the completed application form and all supporting documents through the online application portal.

5

Application Review:

Our admissions committee will thoroughly review each application and make informed decisions based on the applicant's qualifications and potential.

6

Admission Decision:

All applicants will be notified of their admission status within the specified timeline. Accepted students will receive further instructions to complete the enrollment process.

VISIT US
We invite prospective students and their families to visit VNHS and experience our vibrant campus firsthand. Attend our open house events or schedule a personalized tour to explore our facilities, meet our faculty, and get a sense of the VNHS community spirit.
